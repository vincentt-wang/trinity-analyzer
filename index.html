引擎怎麼想How the engine thinks

三個天體，三個問題Three bodies, three questions

一個可投資的新創，三件事要同時成立。少一個，系統都會告訴你。An investable startup needs three things to hold at once. Miss one and the system tells you.

地球・欲求性Earth · Desirability

有人真的要嗎Does anyone want it

從人出發。我們的家、我們的市場，痛點夠真、夠多人有，他們才會用、會付錢。Start from people. Our home, our market: is the pain real enough and shared by enough of them to use and pay.

「這是不是一個真實的需求？」"Is this a real need?"
月亮・可行性Moon · Feasibility

你做得起來嗎Can you build it

引力讓系統運作。技術、團隊、資源到不到位，機制能不能實際跑起來。Gravity keeps the system running. Tech, team and resources: can the mechanism actually operate.

「你有能力把它做出來嗎？」"Can you actually make it work?"
太陽・存續性Sun · Viability

活得下去嗎Will it last

太陽供應持續的能量。商業模式能不能自己產生養分，讓這個物種延續。The Sun supplies steady energy. Can the business model generate its own fuel and let the venture endure.

「它能持續賺錢活下去嗎？」"Can it sustain itself and last?"
How the engine computes引擎的運作原理

Three lenses, fifty-five dimensions, one verdict三個透鏡，五十五個維度，一個判斷

Each lens scores in isolation first, blind to the others, to avoid anchoring. Then each blooms into the dimensions that compose it, fifty-five solid algorithms in all, cross-links in six directions, and converges into one verdict. Hover any dimension; click a lens to isolate it.三個透鏡先各自獨立評分，誰也看不到誰，避免互相錨定。接著每個綻開成組成它的維度，總共五十五套扎實演算法，六向交叉，再匯流成一個判斷。滑過任一維度，點任一透鏡可單獨聚焦。

STEP 01

你只要填幾格，或丟一份 deckFill a few fields, or drop a deck

一句話、問題、目前進展、怎麼賺錢。系統會把它整理成可分析的結構，缺什麼會告訴你。One-liner, problem, traction, model. The system structures it and tells you what's missing.

STEP 02

先建事實庫，分清楚已知與待查It builds a fact base first

每個說法被標成已知、待驗證、或缺口。自述沒來源的數字，不會被當成事實。Every claim is tagged known, to-verify, or gap. Self-reported numbers are not taken as fact.

STEP 03

三個透鏡獨立打分，再交叉連動Three lenses score, then cross-check

欲求性、可行性、存續性各自獨立評，避免互相錨定，再看它們怎麼互相拉扯。Desirability, Feasibility, Viability score independently to avoid anchoring, then we see how they pull on each other.

STEP 04

合成一個你能行動的判斷A verdict you can act on

不是一個分數，是一句話：你卡在哪、為什麼還沒被否決、補哪一塊會升級。Not a score, a sentence: where you're stuck, why it's not vetoed, and which fix levels you up.

STEP 05

配對到對的階段、對的人Matched to the right stage and people

依你的階段與最強的透鏡，配對台美兩地對的機構，並標明是公開申請還是要引薦。By your stage and strongest lens, matched to the right US and Taiwan institutions, marked open-apply or warm-intro.

STEP 06

拿到報告、改進清單與聯絡Report, fix list and contacts

一份指出哪裡要修的報告，附機構官網與聯絡，可匯出 PDF／Word，寄到你的信箱。A report that names what to fix, with institution sites and contacts, exportable to PDF/Word, emailed to you.
