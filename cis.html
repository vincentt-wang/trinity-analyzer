Optimization plan

How we push it to the top tier

The design system is also a roadmap. These are the moves, in priority order, that take Trinity from strong to awwwards-tier and launch-ready.

01

One transform owner per element P1

Route all motion through GSAP (quickTo) so CSS transitions, scrub and pointer transforms never fight. Removes the last of the "not-smooth" feel.

02

Engine + math as the signature P1

The independent-then-cross animation and the Monte-Carlo / tornado models are the moat made visible. Keep them front-and-center, performant, and gated to viewport.

03

Investor-fit as the payoff P1

The closing section animates the recommendation: stage, investor type, named US + Taiwan institutions with logos, contact links, how to approach, exit path.

04

One cohesive cosmos P2

Single starfield + aurora behind every page and doc; translucent glass panels so the universe shows through. No section feels different.

05

Mobile parity P2

Reduced 3D pixel ratio, autoplay timelines instead of pin-scrub, sticky-bottom CTAs, single-column grids. Verified on real devices.

06

Launch motion P3

Pair the site with the video / self-media engine: hero loop as a teaser clip, the engine animation as an explainer reel, ship a waitlist + first free-round funnel.
